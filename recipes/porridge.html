yam porridge

Description

Yam is the major component of this dish, the right yam must be chosen at all times

To get the correct result, you need to pay attention to details of the recipe

Ingredients

  • yam
  • pepper
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • pumpkin leaf
  • crayfish
  • dried fish
  • seasoning
  • salt
  • water
  • palm oil

Steps

  1. peel the yam and dissect into small units
  2. wash/rinse multiple times, then put in a pot
  3. add water in the pot and let it boil for 15 minutes
  4. meanwhile grate your pepper,tomato and onion
  5. after 15 minutes, add salt, pepper mixture, crafish, dried fish and cook for 5 minutes
  6. add seasoning and washed pumpkin
  7. add palm oil and leave to steam
  8. you are good to go
